Inside a compact arena filled with rival players, survival depends on sharp timing and spatial awareness. Each player begins with a small spinning blade setup and must navigate carefully to avoid stronger opponents. Eliminating rivals increases size and reach, making your blade more dangerous but also turning you into a bigger target. The fast respawn system ensures constant action, while skillful dodging and well-timed throws create intense competitive moments.

Gameplay focuses on arena survival through movement precision and tactical aggression. Players must circle opponents carefully, waiting for the right opportunity to strike.

As you eliminate more rivals, your blade radius expands, increasing attack range. However, larger size demands smarter positioning to avoid coordinated threats. The simple mechanics allow easy entry, while mastery requires strong reaction timing and map awareness.
